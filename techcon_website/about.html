History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 50 technology enthusiasts in a university auditorium. Founded by a group of passionate developers who wanted to create a platform for sharing knowledge and fostering innovation, the conference has grown exponentially over the years.

By 2015, TechCon had expanded to accommodate over 1,000 attendees and began featuring international speakers. The conference became known for its cutting-edge presentations on emerging technologies and its commitment to inclusivity in the tech industry.

Today, TechCon stands as one of the premier technology conferences globally, attracting over 5,000 participants from 40+ countries and featuring more than 100 speakers across multiple tracks covering AI, cybersecurity, web development, and emerging technologies.

Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to cutting-edge technology knowledge and create meaningful connections within the global tech community. We believe that innovation thrives when diverse minds collaborate and share ideas freely.

TechCon is committed to:

  • Providing world-class educational content that empowers attendees to advance their careers and projects
  • Fostering an inclusive environment where all voices are heard and valued
  • Bridging the gap between academic research and practical industry applications
  • Supporting underrepresented groups in technology through scholarships and mentorship programs
  • Promoting ethical technology development and responsible innovation

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential voices in technology. Our speakers include industry pioneers, research leaders, and innovative entrepreneurs who shape the future of technology.

Dr. Sarah Chen

Chief AI Researcher at Global Tech Solutions, Dr. Chen spoke at TechCon 2023 about the ethical implications of artificial intelligence in healthcare. With over 15 years of experience in machine learning and medical AI, she has published 50+ peer-reviewed papers and holds 12 patents in AI-driven diagnostic systems.

Marcus Rodriguez

Founder and CTO of SecureNet Innovations, Marcus delivered a groundbreaking keynote on cybersecurity trends in 2022. His company has protected over 1,000 organizations from cyber threats, and he is recognized as one of the top 40 cybersecurity experts under 40.

Dr. Amara Okafor

Professor of Computer Science at Cambridge University and pioneer in quantum computing research. Dr. Okafor's 2021 presentation on quantum algorithms revolutionized how attendees think about computational complexity. She leads a research team of 25 scientists working on practical quantum computing applications.

Raj Patel

Senior Software Engineer at OpenSource Foundation and creator of three widely-used JavaScript libraries. Raj's 2020 workshop on modern web development frameworks attracted over 500 participants and is still referenced by developers worldwide. He has contributed to over 50 open-source projects with millions of downloads.
