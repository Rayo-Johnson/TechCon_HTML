Conference Overview

Join us for three days of cutting-edge technology discussions, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities. The conference runs from March 15-17, 2024, featuring multiple tracks covering AI, web development, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

Day 2 & 3 Highlights

Day 2 focuses on hands-on workshops including "Building Your First AI Application," "Advanced CSS Techniques," and "DevOps Best Practices." Day 3 features startup pitches, career development sessions, and the closing ceremony with special guest announcements for TechCon 2025.

Note: All sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees. Workshop materials and code samples will be provided via our conference app.
